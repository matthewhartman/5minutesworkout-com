5 Minutes Workout - Privacy Policy

Overview

5 Minutes Workout (5minutesworkout.com) is designed for everyone, with special attention to online privacy and a lot of care has been taken to be COPPA and GDPR compliant.

5 Minutes Workout does not collect any personal information.

The term "personal information" refers to personally identifiable information through which a user can be identified as an individual. The term "Non-personal information" refers to information that does not directly identify a user.

Collection and Use of Personal Information

Any of your personal information is not collected through 5 Minutes Workout.

Collection and Use of Non-Personal Information

Your non-personal information via 5 Minutes Workout is not collected and no tools or third-party analytical software is used to gather or analyze non-personal information.

The 5 Minutes Workout does not display third-party advertising nor does it use push notifications or collect location data.

Collection of Data

5 Minutes Workout does not knowingly collect any personal information via our website. If you believe there has been a mistake in collecting such information, please contact the developer via email: matthewhartmanau@gmail.com to resolve the issue.

If you have any questions or concerns about the above privacy policy, please contact the developer via email: matthewhartmanau@gmail.com
